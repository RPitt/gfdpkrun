ULTRAK 499 / DIGISport DT2000 StopWatch Guide

Clearing Stored Times

  • Press MODE (C) until you are on the timing screen with 3 lines of times.
    If the number is less than 2000 there are times to clear.
  • Press RECALL (D), you should now see MCH at the bottom left of the screen.
  • Hold down START/STOP (A) and continue holding until ALL CLEARED is displayed and the beeping has stopped.

Timing

  • Press MODE (C) until you are on the timing screen.
  • Check the screen says 2000 ⇒ if not see “Clearing Stored Times” above.
  • Check the screen says SPLIT CHRONO ⇒ if not press and hold LAP (B) for 2 seconds to change mode.
  • Press START/STOP (A) to start the run.
  • Press LAP (B) for every runner crossing the finish.
  • Press START/STOP (A) after the run.
  • Press LAP (B) to store the times.

Uploading

  • Press MODE (C) until you see UPLOAD ALL.
  • If the stopwatch says ‘PRINT ALL’ ⇒ press RECALL (D) to change to upload mode.
  • If the stopwatch says ‘UPLOAD 0’ ⇒ you haven’t stored the times, see the last step above!
  • Assuming you’re connected to the computer with the software ready… press START/STOP (A) to start the upload.
